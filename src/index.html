OUR STORY

Heather and Dave met each other via a mutual and dear friend- Farrah Fine circa 2007. The initial introduction happened outside of Cotopaxi, CO, on the banks of the Arkansas River. They ran the Salt River in Arizona and Hells Canyon of the Snake River in Idaho in 2010. Thereafter, they stayed in touch and planned a fateful trip down the Colorado River winding through Ruby-Horsethief and Westwater Canyon in April 2012. After that trip, something had changed.

This change prompted Dave to send Heather a package to prepare her for her Fall 2012 voyage down The Grand Canyon of the Colorado River. Now, sending mail parcels to boaters on the river is a process. It involves teams of jackasses and mules, perilous trails, and brutal heat. Unfortunately, Dave's package of brandy, lip balm, whiskey, pirate eye patches, and a banana suit didn't get picked up during Heather's river trip. The package was diverted back to Heather's home in Durango, where hilarity ensued upon its arrival. Heather wore her pirate eye patch for a whole week!

After the package arrived, they had a bigger conversation about combining their super powers. Dave was working overseas at the time, so over the phone, they hatched a plan for an epic road trip from South Carolina to the Catskills in May 2013. Heather picked Dave up at the airport in the banana suit to be sure he would recognize her. During this trip, they paddled many rivers and visited friends and family along the way. Dave got to meet Heather’s mom, Grandma Reis, friends from Westford, and some of the Menzie family in the Catskills. In Fall 2013, Dave and Heather took another trip to the Catskills. This was followed up with a November trip to the Bahamas, where Dave chased sea turtles and they got lost under the sea. That Thanksgiving, they traveled to Ohio where Heather met Dave’s family. The rest is history. They now live in Boulder, CO.
